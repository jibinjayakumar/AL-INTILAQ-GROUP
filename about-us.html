About Our Company

Welcome to Al Intilaq Group

With more than 250 professionally qualified and experienced employees on its rolls, the Al lntilaq Group is looked upon as a resilient and reliable company that can handle the toughest jobs in the construction, oil & gas and process industries.

This reputation has been further strengthened by our track record of exceeding client expectations on each project that we have undertaken so far. The company's initial focus was on general contracting, executing civil and mechanical engineering projects of varying magnitudes.

Today Al lntilaq supports local, state, and federal government departments, oil majors, and international consortiums within UAE and abroad, providing unique capabilities and performing niche assignments. Our clients today include various Port Authorities, as well as local and international firms.

What sets Al lntilaq apart is its ability to foresee the immediate as well as long term growth trajectories of various industrial sectors, to understand the challenges that lay ahead while pursuing those trajectories, and to gear itself up to meet those challenges head on. This foresight and advance preparedness gives Al Intilaq a leg up over the competition.

MISSION

Our mission is to help organizations in completing critical projects within schedule and within budget while maintaining the highest quality standards by deploying highly motivated, committed, and flexible teams, with every team member focused on providing our customers with exceptional workmanship, extraordinary service, and professional integrity.

VISION

Our vision is to be the preeminent provider of quality workforce for the construction, interior designing, oil & gas, process industries to add value for clients through innovation, foresight, integrity, and aggressive performance and to serve with character and purpose that brings honor to God. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.

QUALITY

Al lntilaq is committed to delivering high quality service to its customers. The Company is focused on completing projects in accordance with agreed schedules, specifications and cost, meeting the highest standards of quality and efficiency whilst ensuring that statutory and regulatory requirements are complied with, and Client expectations are fully met. To this end, the Company has established Quality objectives that are regularly reviewed for continued suitability. Employees are encouraged to take pride in, and responsibility for their work and to develop better working practices as part of our drive towards continual improvement.

Manpower Recruitment Division

Al Intilaq is a leader in innovative workforce solutions, connecting human capital to businesses both large and small, across various industry verticals. Having top quality employees on the rolls is a prime requirement for any successful business. The need for greater productivity coupled with increased competitiveness has underlined the critical role that people play in the success of an organization. However, finding the right candidate with the requisite knowledge, qualifications, experience and soft skills is both challenging, and time-consuming. That's where Al lntilaq comes in.

Al lntilaq has been in the business of mobilizing manpower for numerous industries, business houses and services organizations for over a decade. Al Intilaq has been providing quality personnel in all categories — from semi-skilled workers to very highly qualified, experienced and dynamic individuals to fill the upper echelons of an organization's hierarchy.
