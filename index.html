About Us

With more than 250 professionally qualified and experienced employees on its rolls, the Al lntilaq Group is looked upon as a resilient and reliable company that can handle the toughest jobs in the construction, oil & gas and process industries.

This reputation has been further strengthened by our track record of exceeding client expectations on each project that we have undertaken so far. The company's initial focus was on general contracting, executing civil and mechanical engineering projects of varying magnitudes.

Today Al lntilaq supports local, state, and federal government departments, oil majors, and international consortiums within UAE and abroad, providing unique capabilities and performing niche assignments. Our clients today include various Port Authorities, as well as local and international firms.
